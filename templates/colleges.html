Colleges Page

Hi! Our project seeks to advise high school students like ourselves into picking a college that best suits them. We all know how life changing this decision could be, and picking a school that is right for you could lead to a students success in their future careers, lifestyles, and attitudes toward education.

Some Things We Will Ask About You:

  • Information about your work and study habits
  • Your interests as an individual and student
  • What you are looking for in terms of location and environment
  • What you see yourself doing in the future

    • Some Feedback We Would Love to Hear:

  • What other questions do you think would lead to a more accurate choice for you?
  • Are there any colleges missing that we should know about and add?
